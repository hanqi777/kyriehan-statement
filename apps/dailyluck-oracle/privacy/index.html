Privacy Policy

DailyLuck Oracle Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 19, 2026. This page explains how DailyLuck Oracle handles profile details, daily fortune records, settings, purchases, and sharing features.

1. Summary

DailyLuck Oracle is designed as a local-first daily fortune and reflection app. Its core profile, daily fortune, recent trend, history, language, and sharing features are intended to run on the user's device.

We do not sell personal data. We do not use your birthday, optional birth hour, profile details, fortune history, or app activity for advertising or tracking.

2. Information DailyLuck Oracle may store locally

Depending on how you use the app, DailyLuck Oracle may store:

  • Your profile name entered in the app
  • Your solar birthday selected in the app
  • Your gender selection, if selected
  • Your optional birth hour, if selected
  • Your selected focus areas, such as career, money, love, health, inspiration, or overall luck
  • Daily fortune results, history, lucky color, lucky number, category scores, and trend data generated by the app
  • Language preference and app settings
  • Local purchase access state used to unlock Pro features

This information is stored on your device to provide the app's features. DailyLuck Oracle does not require an account.

3. How information is used

Information is used only to provide app features, such as:

  • Creating and saving your local fortune profile
  • Generating an offline daily fortune and fortune history
  • Showing recent and future luck trends
  • Displaying lucky color, lucky number, keyword, and category scores
  • Switching Chinese and English content based on your language setting
  • Preparing a share image if you choose to share your fortune
  • Unlocking Pro features after a valid App Store purchase

4. Local processing, storage, and sharing

DailyLuck Oracle generates readings offline on the device. The app does not upload your profile, birthday, optional birth hour, fortune history, or generated readings to our servers.

If you choose to share a fortune image through iOS share features, the selected destination handles that sharing under its own terms and privacy practices.

If you delete the app, locally stored app data may be removed by iOS. You can also edit your local profile inside the app.

5. Purchases

DailyLuck Oracle may offer a one-time Pro purchase. Purchases, refunds, billing details, taxes, and payment information are handled by Apple through the App Store.

The app uses Apple's StoreKit system to check purchase eligibility and restore purchases. We do not receive your full payment card details.

6. Permissions

DailyLuck Oracle is designed to work without camera, microphone, location, contacts, or photo library access for its core fortune features.

If a future version introduces a feature that requires additional permissions, the app will request permission through iOS before using that feature, and this policy will be updated as needed.

7. No tracking or advertising

DailyLuck Oracle does not use third-party advertising SDKs and does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

If future versions introduce analytics, cloud sync, accounts, advertising, or external services, this policy will be updated before or with the relevant release.

8. Entertainment and professional advice notice

DailyLuck Oracle is provided for entertainment, journaling, and self-reflection only. It does not provide medical, legal, financial, psychological, or other professional advice. Please use your own judgment for real-life decisions and consult qualified professionals when needed.

9. Children's privacy

DailyLuck Oracle is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Policy updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page and will apply to future app releases.
